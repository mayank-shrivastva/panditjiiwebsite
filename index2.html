Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in puja?

Each puja includes mantra chanting, vidhi by experienced Pandit Ji, basic samagri (if selected), and step-by-step rituals.

What cities do you serve?

We currently serve Delhi, Mumbai, Pune, Lucknow, Jaipur and many more. Online pujas are available pan-India.

Can I cancel?

Yes, you can cancel up to 24 hours before the scheduled puja. Refund or rescheduling options are available.
