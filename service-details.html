üî± Pind Daan Puja in Haridwar

Pind Daan is one of the most sacred rituals in Sanatan Dharma, performed to bring peace and salvation to the souls of our ancestors (Pitrs). It is believed that without performing proper Pind Daan, the souls remain restless and cannot attain Moksha (liberation). Haridwar ‚Äì where the Ganga meets the Himalayas ‚Äì is considered one of the most powerful and auspicious sites for this ritual, as per scriptures like the Garuda Purana and Vayu Purana.

According to Hindu beliefs, performing Pind Daan at Har Ki Pauri and other sacred ghats in Haridwar ensures that the departed soul finds a place in Pitrulok and eventually achieves freedom from the cycle of rebirth. The ritual involves offering rice balls (pindas), til (sesame), and water to the ancestors along with Vedic mantras chanted by learned Brahmins.

Our team ensures that every Pind Daan ceremony is done with utmost devotion, proper vidhi (method), and spiritual discipline. From arranging a certified Vedic Pandit Ji to providing all authentic puja samagri, we take care of everything so that you can focus on your prayers and rituals.

Whether you are coming to Haridwar in person or staying abroad, we offer both on-site and online Pind Daan services. For families who cannot travel due to age, health, or distance, our pandits will perform the rituals on your behalf and send you full video/photo documentation for complete transparency.

The entire puja is done as per your gotra, tithi, and family tradition. You can consult with our priests beforehand and select the appropriate date. We also offer flexibility for special occasions like Amavasya, Pitru Paksha, Shraddh Paksha, or barsi (death anniversary).

Join thousands of satisfied families who have performed their ancestral rituals with us. We are committed to delivering a spiritually fulfilling experience with complete authenticity, compassion, and professionalism. Our goal is to help you connect with your roots and fulfill your duties towards your forefathers as per Hindu Dharma.
